Scrum Lake guides and facilitators

Scrum Lake has been created by John Albrecht and Ryan Brook as a way to dive deeper into Scrum using the power of metaphor. They are both experienced Scrum Masters who love working with teams and helping them create a culture of continued improvement. Despite calling themselves Scrum Masters in their day to day jobs, here they call themselves Dive Masters - they help our workshop participants navigate through the Lake by facilitating deep discussions. Even though they are the facilitators, they are equally active participants in the journey. Their favourite part of Scrum Lake is meeting with other experienced practitioners and sharing ideas.

If you'd like to find out about our Dive Masters, you can take a look at their brief introductions below. However, they'd much prefer you to send them an email or message on their social media to create a valuable connection.

John Albrecht PSM III

John is a Scrum Master and coach with a long and varied experience of growing Agile teams. He has a technical background and grows teams using value-based principles. He is a leader who serves, creating the transparency needed for others to identify how to move forward. He is a noted speaker, appearing at numerous Agile conferences and webinars and is perhaps best known for his audiobook of the current edition of the Scrum Guide. Alongside working with teams, he provides one to one coaching and mentoring for Scrum Masters, Product Owners and Agile leaders.

Ryan Brook PSM III | PSPO III

Ryan spends his time working with teams and organisations to change their mindset from 'delivery at whatever cost' to 'value at whatever cost'. Whilst he refers to himself as a Scrum Master, he isn't wedded to one particular framework. He uses creativity and a positive attitude to affect change and encourages people to find what works for them. Improving organisational culture is the backbone of Ryan's practice. Without it, he believes teams are not able to reap the benefits of an empirical framework.
