General questions

How can I get involved in Scrum Lake?

We run regular meetups to explore Scrum Lake. Keep an eye on our 'Upcoming Workshops' page for registration details. They are interactive events and so are not recorded. The only way to participate is to get involved!

Can I contribute to the development of Scrum Lake?

Currently, Scrum Lake is not open source. However, if after attending one of our events you have some feedback for us to improve, please let us know and we may add it to our Product Backlog.

How much does it cost to attend a meetup?

At the moment, all of the events are free to attend. The tools we use (Miro and Zoom) do not require any paid subscriptions and therefore the only cost is your time (although we're sure you'll get a good return on that investment!). Having said this, the interest has been incredibly high and so we may start to charge a small fee in future.
