Dive into Scrum, Co-Creative Workshops

For folks interested in Scrum, Scrum Lake is a place to share stories, discuss techniques and take a deeper dive into the Scrum. Work with Scrum Masters and Coaches from around the world and uncover a much deeper understanding through metaphor

Scrum Lake workshops have received many plaudits, due to the our different approach, taking folks on a journey to explore together.

Share, Collaborate and Grow

Scrum Lake workshops are a safe, structured environment for Scrum enthusiasts to be able to share stories, collaborate and learn effective techniques to deliver positive change. Scrum Lake is just the place you are looking for to co-create your own exploration of deeper Scrum, facilitated by two experienced Scrum Masters. No matter what your 'level' of Scrum knowledge currently is, you are very welcome to join us and Dive into our Lake to share, collaborate and grow.

You will find Scrum Lake Workshops to very interactive experience. Our lake canvas, breakouts, stories, metaphor in a remote first setting ensure that variety of thought are surfaced and explored together.

Come Join us

Our Workshops are scheduled every few weeks, each workshop is unique owing to the diversity of attendees and scrum stories from around the world. You and your fellow divers will explore together. Do you want to dive deep into theory? Stay shallow and share stories? Whatever you want, we explore together.

Come and join us on our next adventure.
