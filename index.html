Dive in, co-create your own journey.

For professionals interested in the Scrum framework, Scrum Lake is a place to share stories, discuss techniques and take a deeper dive into the Scrum Guide.

Our mission.

We want to create a safe, structured environment for Scrum enthusiasts to be able to share stories, collaborate and learn effective techniques to deliver positive change. Scrum Lake will be a place to co-create your own exploration of Scrum, facilitated by experienced Scrum Masters. No matter what your 'level' of Scrum knowledge is, you are welcome.

Join us on our next adventure.

Workshops are run frequently and every single one is different! That is the beauty of Scrum Lake. You and your fellow collaborators will explore together. Do you want to dive deep into theory? Stay shallow and share stories? Whatever you want, it's up to you and your team.

Come and join us on our next adventure.
