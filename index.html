Dive in Co-Create your own experience

For professionals interested in the Scrum framework, Scrum Lake is a place to share stories, discuss techniques and take a deeper dive into the Scrum Guide.

Our mission

We want to create a safe, structured environment for Scrum enthusiasts to be able to share stories, collaborate and learn effective techniques to effect positive change. Scrum Lake will be a place to co-create your own exploration of Scrum, facilitated by experienced Scrum Masters. No matter what your 'level' of Scrum knowledge is, you are welcome.

Join us on our next adventure

Workshops are run frequently, and every single one is different! That is the beauty of Scrum Lake. Your fellow collaborators will explore together. Do you want to dive deep into theory? Or stay shallow and share stories? Whatever you want, it's up to you.
