Dive in, Co-Creative Workshops

For people interested in Scrum, Scrum Lake is a place to share stories, discuss techniques and take a deeper dive into Scrum theory, practice and reality. We facilitate Scrum Masters and coaches from around the world to deepen their understanding of the framework and improve their explanations of key concepts.

We are proud that Scrum Lake workshops are consistently positively received. We believe this is due to a unique exploratory approach that encourages new ways of thinking.

Share, collaborate and grow

Scrum Lake workshops are a safe and structured environment for Scrum enthusiasts to be able to discuss stories and work together to learn effective techniques to deliver positive change. They provide you with the opportunity to co-create your own exploration of deeper Scrum, facilitated by two experienced Scrum Masters. No matter what your 'level' of Scrum knowledge currently is, you are very welcome to join us and dive into our Lake to share, collaborate and grow.

You will find our workshops to be a very interactive experience. The regular breakouts and story sharing, all bounded by supportive metaphors, help ensure that varieties of thought and opinion are surfaced and explored together.

Come Join us

The workshops are scheduled every few weeks and each workshop is unique owing to the diversity of attendees and stories from around the World. You and your fellow divers will explore together. Do you want to dive deep into theory? Stay shallow and share stories? Whatever you want, we explore together.

Come and join us on our next adventure.
