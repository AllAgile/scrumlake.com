Co-Creative Workshops dive into Scrum

For folks interested in Scrum, Scrum Lake is a place to share stories, discuss techniques and take a deeper dive into the Scrum. Work with Scrum Masters and Coaches from around the world and uncover a much deeper understanding through metaphor
Scrum Lake workshops have received many plaudits, due to the very different approach and they tend to sell out quickly.

Share, Collaborate and Grow

Scrum Lake workshops are a safe, structured environment for Scrum enthusiasts to be able to share stories, collaborate and learn effective techniques to deliver positive change. Scrum Lake will be a place to co-create your own exploration of Scrum, facilitated by experienced Scrum Masters. No matter what your 'level' of Scrum knowledge is, you are very welcome to Dive into our Lake.
You will find Scrum Lake Workshops to very interactive. Our lake canvas, breakouts, stories, metaphor in a remote first setting ensure that variety of thought are surfaced and explored.

Come Join us

Our Workshops are scheduled every few weeks, each workshop is unique owing to the diversity of attendees and scrum stories from around the world. You and your fellow divers will explore together. Do you want to dive deep into theory? Stay shallow and share stories? Whatever you want, we explore together.

Come and join us on our next adventure.
